Job Posting

2026-03-18

The "agentic" coding hype is getting a bit crazy. I recieved a spam email with this job posting in it this week:

Agentic first software engineer - Vibe coder / Claude Coder - Yolo expert Wonder Dog United States Remote $125,000 - $200,000 a year - Full-time

I never expected to need to fact check through job openings with Yolo in them. Best I can tell Wonder Dog does not have a real website, and hopefully is not a real company but their posting is certainly on Indeed getting circulation.
